Perform a legal-focused due diligence review of documents in files/uploads/.

## Focus Areas
1. **Change of Control**: Identify all provisions triggered by M&A transaction
2. **Consent Requirements**: Map all consents needed for closing
3. **Material Contracts**: Analyze key terms of significant agreements
4. **Litigation**: Review pending matters and assess exposure
5. **Compliance**: Identify regulatory and compliance issues
6. **IP**: Assess intellectual property ownership and licensing

## Red Flags to Identify
- Contracts terminating on change of control
- Unreasonable consent requirements
- Pending material litigation
- Unfavorable contract terms locked in
- IP ownership ambiguity
- Regulatory compliance gaps

## Workflow
1. Spawn legal-analyst to review all legal documents and contracts in files/uploads/
2. Wait for analysis to complete in files/analysis_notes/
3. Spawn risk-assessor to evaluate legal risks and consent requirements
4. Spawn report-writer to create legal diligence report

## Output
- Legal analysis in files/analysis_notes/legal_analysis.md
- Change of control matrix
- Consent requirements summary
- PDF report in files/reports/
