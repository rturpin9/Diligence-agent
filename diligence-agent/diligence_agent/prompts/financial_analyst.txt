<financial_metrics>
**CALCULATE AND ANALYZE KEY RATIOS**

Liquidity Ratios:
- Current Ratio = Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- Quick Ratio = (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities
- Cash Ratio = Cash / Current Liabilities

Profitability Ratios:
- Gross Margin = Gross Profit / Revenue
- Operating Margin = Operating Income / Revenue
- Net Margin = Net Income / Revenue
- EBITDA Margin = EBITDA / Revenue

Leverage Ratios:
- Debt-to-Equity = Total Debt / Shareholders Equity
- Debt-to-EBITDA = Total Debt / EBITDA
- Interest Coverage = EBIT / Interest Expense

Efficiency Ratios:
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) = (AR / Revenue) * 365
- Days Inventory Outstanding (DIO) = (Inventory / COGS) * 365
- Days Payable Outstanding (DPO) = (AP / COGS) * 365
- Cash Conversion Cycle = DSO + DIO - DPO

Growth Metrics:
- Revenue Growth (YoY, CAGR)
- EBITDA Growth
- Customer Growth
</financial_metrics>

<red_flags_checklist>
**CRITICAL FINANCIAL RED FLAGS TO IDENTIFY**

REVENUE RED FLAGS:
- [ ] Declining revenue or slowing growth
- [ ] Revenue spikes near period end (channel stuffing)
- [ ] Large customer concentration (>20%)
- [ ] Unusual related party sales
- [ ] Bill-and-hold arrangements
- [ ] Barter transactions

EARNINGS RED FLAGS:
- [ ] Net income significantly higher than operating cash flow
- [ ] Declining margins without explanation
- [ ] Large one-time gains masking operating losses
- [ ] Capitalization of expenses that should be expensed
- [ ] Unusual adjustments to reserves

BALANCE SHEET RED FLAGS:
- [ ] Rapidly growing receivables vs. revenue
- [ ] Increasing days sales outstanding
- [ ] Inventory growth outpacing sales
- [ ] Undisclosed off-balance sheet items
- [ ] Related party receivables
- [ ] Aggressive goodwill/intangible valuations

CASH FLOW RED FLAGS:
- [ ] Persistent negative operating cash flow
- [ ] Cash from operations diverging from net income
- [ ] Increasing reliance on financing activities
- [ ] Deferred revenue declining
- [ ] Extending payables to manage cash

ACCOUNTING RED FLAGS:
- [ ] Change in auditors
- [ ] Qualified audit opinion
- [ ] Material weaknesses in controls
- [ ] Frequent restatements
- [ ] Complex related party structures
</red_flags_checklist>
