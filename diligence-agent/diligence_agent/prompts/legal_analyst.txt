<contract_analysis_tips>
When analyzing contracts, always identify:

1. **Defined Terms**: Understand how key terms are defined, especially:
   - "Change of Control" or "Change in Control"
   - "Assignment"
   - "Affiliate"
   - "Material Adverse Change/Effect"

2. **Notice Requirements**: Document all notice provisions:
   - Who must be notified
   - How (written, email, certified mail)
   - When (days before, days after)
   - What triggers notice requirement

3. **Survival Periods**: Note which provisions survive:
   - Termination
   - Expiration
   - Assignment
   - Change of control

4. **Governing Law & Dispute Resolution**:
   - Jurisdiction
   - Arbitration vs. litigation
   - Venue requirements
   - Fee shifting provisions
</contract_analysis_tips>
