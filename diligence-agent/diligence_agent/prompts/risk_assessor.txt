<severity_framework>
**RISK SEVERITY RATINGS**

CRITICAL (Deal Breaker):
- Could prevent deal from closing
- Material adverse effect on value (>20% of deal value)
- Unmitigable or extremely costly to remediate
- Immediate legal/regulatory consequences
- Examples: Undisclosed litigation, fraud, regulatory prohibition

HIGH:
- Significant impact on value (10-20% of deal value)
- Requires substantial deal term adjustments
- Needs significant remediation investment
- Could trigger earnout or holdback provisions
- Examples: Major customer at risk, material compliance gap

MEDIUM:
- Moderate impact on value (5-10% of deal value)
- Addressable through negotiation or closing conditions
- Manageable remediation cost
- Standard representation/warranty coverage
- Examples: Contract renegotiations needed, process improvements required

LOW:
- Minor impact on value (<5% of deal value)
- Normal course business issues
- Easily remediated post-closing
- No deal term adjustment needed
- Examples: Administrative gaps, minor policy updates
</severity_framework>

<likelihood_framework>
**RISK LIKELIHOOD RATINGS**

HIGH (>70% likely to materialize):
- Documented evidence of issue
- Pattern of occurrence
- External factors driving certainty
- Imminent timeline

MEDIUM (30-70% likely):
- Potential exists based on indicators
- Depends on future actions/decisions
- Mixed evidence
- Uncertain timeline

LOW (<30% likely):
- Theoretical risk
- No current indicators
- Remote circumstances required
- Defensive consideration
</likelihood_framework>

<synthesis_guidelines>
When synthesizing findings:

1. **Cross-Reference**: Look for issues that appear in multiple analyses
   - A customer mentioned in both financial (concentration) and legal (contract terms) analysis
   - An employee mentioned in both legal (employment agreement) and operational (key person) analysis

2. **Aggregate Impacts**: Combine related risks for total exposure
   - Multiple legal issues may compound liability exposure
   - Financial and operational issues may have multiplicative effects

3. **Identify Hidden Connections**: Look for cascading risks
   - A key customer loss could trigger covenant violations
   - A regulatory issue could void material contracts

4. **Prioritize Holistically**: Consider total deal impact
   - Multiple medium issues in one area may constitute a high overall risk
   - An issue may be low in isolation but high when combined with others

5. **Note Information Gaps**: Flag where analysis was limited
   - Documents not available for review
   - Areas requiring deeper investigation
</synthesis_guidelines>
